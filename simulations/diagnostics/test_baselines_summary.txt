# Baseline Methods Test Results (2026-03-04)

## Test Setup
- DGP: Binary (4 features, n=400, τ=0.10)
- Methods: Forest-DML (ranger), Linear-DML (GLM)
- Replications: 100
- Cross-fitting: K=5 folds

## Results

### Forest-DML (ranger)
- ✓ Runs without errors
- Bias: 0.0356 (moderate, ~36% of truth)
- RMSE: 0.0645
- Coverage: 74% ⚠️ (target: 95%)
- Mean CI width: 0.138

### Linear-DML (GLM)
- ✓ Runs without errors
- Bias: 0.0067 (small, ~7% of truth)
- RMSE: 0.0472
- Coverage: 82% ⚠️ (target: 95%)
- Mean CI width: 0.133

## Issues Identified

**Undercoverage in both methods:** CIs are too narrow, suggesting:
1. Variance estimation formula may be simplified
2. EIF-based variance may need cross-fitting adjustment
3. Need to compare with dmltree's variance calculation

## Status

✓ **Methods are functional** (no crashes, reasonable estimates)
